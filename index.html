FAQ

Clear answers for buyers.

Is this for personal finance or business finance?

Both. It covers household tracking while also including business-friendly tools like reports, ledgers, vendors, journal entries, and reconciliation.

What makes it different from a spreadsheet?

It gives users structured accounts, categorized transactions, budgets, recurring entries, and reports without manual spreadsheet maintenance.

Can it support professional-looking financial snapshots?

Yes. The app includes Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet reporting for understanding income, expenses, assets, liabilities, equity, and net worth.
